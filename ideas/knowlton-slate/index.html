What the lab is working on

active streams
BREAST CANCER
Can a risk model survive messy screening data?
Multi-modality imaging evaluation in private screening. Nationwide HER2-Low retrospective cohort in Aotearoa NZ. Breast density AI and Wāhine Māori health equity.
policyproductguideline
REPRODUCTIVE AI
Can machine learning rank embryos better than an embryologist?
Morphokinetic stage detection, embryo annotation and segmentation, cohort-relative ranking. The research behind WyldBloom's clinic decision-support tools.
patent-pendingproduct
ADHERENCE
Can we detect adherence failure before it becomes recurrence?
Statistical systems for tracking and improving endocrine therapy adherence in breast cancer patients. BCFNZ registry-linked analysis with clinical collaborators.
policycohort
REPRODUCIBILITY
Can a method be trusted if no one can reproduce it?
Open-source tools and workflows that make translational research auditable — from raw data to published conclusion. Interactive teaching tools for PCA, collinearity and regression.
